WHAT A PROPER INSTALL INCLUDES
There are a lot of corners a roofer can cut where you'll never see them — until a leak shows up three winters later. Here's what's in every shingle job we do, no upsell required:
Full tear-off to the deck
We strip the old roof right down so we can see what we're working with — no shingling over problems.
Deck & rot inspection
We check the wood, replace any soft or rotten sheathing, and tell you before we do.
Ice-and-water shield
At eaves, valleys and penetrations — the single most important defence against Saskatchewan ice damming.
Proper ventilation
Ridge and intake venting so your attic breathes — it's what keeps shingles from cooking off early.
HOW THE JOB GOES
01
Free on-site quote

BUILT FOR SASKATCHEWAN
Forty-below cold snaps, freeze-thaw, July hail and wind off open fields are hard on a roof. We spec heavier architectural shingles, hand-seal in cold weather, and detail the eaves for ice damming — the stuff that actually matters here.
