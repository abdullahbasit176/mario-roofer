SEAMLESS, AND SIZED FOR HERE
Spring melt and prairie cloudbursts move a lot of water fast. Undersized or leaky trough dumps it right against your foundation. We do it properly:
Seamless, formed on site
One continuous run per side means far fewer joints — and joints are where trough leaks.
Sized to your roof
5" or 6" trough and the right number of downspouts for your roof area and pitch.
Drainage away from the house
Downspouts and extensions placed to carry water well clear of your foundation.
Leaf guard options
If you've got mature trees, guards keep the trough clear and cut winter ice build-up.
HOW IT WORKS
01
Free measure & colour pick
We measure your roofline, recommend trough size, and match the colour to your house.
02
Formed & hung on site
We run seamless trough to length right at your place, so it fits clean with minimal joints.
03
Downspouts & clean-up
Downspouts placed for proper drainage, guards added if you want them, site left clean.
SPRING MELT MATTERS
A Saskatoon snowpack melts fast once it turns. Good eavestrough is what keeps that water out of your basement — it's one of the cheapest things you can do to protect your foundation.
